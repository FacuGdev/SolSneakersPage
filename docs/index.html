1 - WHAT IS AN NFT?

NFT stands for "non-fungible token". Broadly speaking, it could be said that it is data that is stored in a digital ledger (blockchain), and that data represents something unique and specific. THROUGHOUT THE WORLD THERE WILL NEVER BE ANOTHER SOL SNEAKERS LIKE YOURS, ISN'T IT GREAT?

2 - WHAT IS THE SOL SNEAKER?

Sol Sneakers represents 5500 algorithmically generated collectible NFT Sneakers, with unique designs and attributes with proof of ownership stored on the Solana blockchain.

3- HOW CAN I BUY IT?

It is very simple, you only need to create a SOL wallet (we recommend Phantom or Sollet)
⠀⠀⠀A. Download the Phantom or Sollet wallet extension for your browser (Google Chrome recommended).
⠀⠀⠀B. Buy Solana (SOL) on an exchange like, Binance, Gate.io, Coinbase, etc. and send it to the public address of your Phantom or Sollet wallet.
⠀⠀⠀C. Connect your Wallet to our website by clicking the Connect button.
⠀⠀⠀D. Click the "Buy" button at https://www.solsneakers.com and approve the transaction in your Wallet.

4 - WHERE DOES MY NFT GO AFTER I BUY IT? HOW CAN I SEE IT?

Your NFT will be sent to your wallet minutes after minting. Click on the NFT section in any of your wallets and you will see your NFT.
